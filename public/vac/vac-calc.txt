Vaccine effectiveness: relative reduction in risk
ve = (not-vac)/not
   = 1 - vac/not
    * vac=not => ve=0%
    * vac=0   => ve=100%

ve* = ((not-bas) - (vac-bas)) / (not-bas)
    = (not-vac)/(not-bas)
    = (not-vac)/not * not/(not-bas)
    = ve * not/(not-bas)

    * vac=bas => ve*=100%
    * vac=not => ve*=0%

    (ILI_not - baseline) - (ILI_vac - baseline) / (ILI_not - baseline)
    = VE * ILI_not/(ILI_not - baseline)
